Today’s relation

Equal-radius intersection. Two circles with the same radius and centres one radius apart produce the conditions for an equilateral triangle.

The image is generated geometry. It is not a stock picture. The plate shows centre, boundary stations, overlapping equal circles, and the central sixfold resolution.

Prompt: draw the first circle, choose one boundary station, keep the radius fixed, and let the second circle reveal the first triangle.
