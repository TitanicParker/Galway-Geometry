Galway Geometry · Colouring Generator

A deterministic lawful colouring engine built from authored recipes. The button may say “Random” later, but the geometry is not arbitrary: each design comes from the triangular lattice, the first circle, six stations, lawful rings, cells, chords, overlaps, and scaled repetitions.

Deterministic reservoir: this build ships with 48 authored recipes and 8 curated seed variants each for 384 lawful designs.

Future site use: the colouring page can call the same recipe and renderer modules, while the “random” button simply selects from the filtered design catalogue.
